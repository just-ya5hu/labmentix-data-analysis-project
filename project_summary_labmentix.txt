
Project Title: Sales Analysis for Business Insights

Objective:
Analyze daily sales and profits across four regions to identify trends, underperforming areas, and potential improvements.

Key Insights:
- West region had the highest total sales.
- Negative profits were observed on 3 days, indicating potential issues in pricing or high costs.
- Profit margin varied significantly, highlighting inconsistency in operational efficiency.

Tools Used:
- Python (pandas, matplotlib)
- Visuals: Bar chart (sales by region), Line chart (daily profit)

Outcome:
This basic analysis shows how data-driven decisions can identify weak areas in a business and improve performance.
